1. Get the API Gateway address:
   kubectl get svc api-gateway -o jsonpath='{.status.loadBalancer.ingress[0].ip}'
   On minikube run: minikube service api-gateway --url

2. Wait for all pods to be ready:
   kubectl get pods -w

3. Point your CLI at the gateway:
   export CICD_GATEWAY_BASE_URL=<address from step 1>
